**🚀 Just completed an advanced data engineering project: "Senegal Development Intelligence Platform"**

This comprehensive analytics platform demonstrates the power of modern data engineering in understanding national development patterns.

**🔧 Technical Stack:**
• **Cloud Data Warehouse**: Snowflake
• **Data Sources**: World Bank API, ANSD Senegal, Enhanced indicators  
• **Dashboard**: Streamlit (Mobile-responsive)
• **Visualization**: Plotly
• **Data Processing**: Python, Pandas

**📊 What I Built:**
✅ **390+ Data Points** across 6 dimensions
✅ **65 Years** of historical data (1960-2024)
✅ **Regional Analysis** - 11 Senegal regions
✅ **Comparative Analysis** - 7 West African countries
✅ **Trend Forecasting** with projections
✅ **Mobile-responsive** dashboard

**🎯 Key Insights Discovered:**
• Population growth: 3.34M → 18.97M (5.7x growth!)
• Urbanization: 23% → 68% (Rapid transformation)
• Economic performance varies significantly by presidential period
• Senegal leads West Africa in urbanization and economic growth

**💡 Business Impact:**
This platform enables data-driven policy decisions, investment analysis, and public awareness through transparent data visualization.
